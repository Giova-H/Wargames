Force Rules

Mission Tactics: At the beginning of the first battle round, choose a datasheet to be your kill team’s priority target (e.g. Tyranid Warrior). You can re-roll wound rolls of 1 for attacks made by models in your kill team that target a model from the datasheet that is your kill team’s priority target (so if you chose Tyranid Warrior, this ability would apply to attacks that targeted Tyranid Warriors and Tyranid Warrior Gunners).
