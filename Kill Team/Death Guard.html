Force Rules

Inexorable Advance: Models in your kill team do not suffer the penalty to their hit rolls for shooting Heavy weapons during a battle round in which they moved, or for shooting Assault weapons during a battle round in which they Advanced. In addition, models in your kill team firing Rapid Fire weapons double the number of attacks they make if all of their targets are within 18" (rather than half the weapon's Range characteristic).
